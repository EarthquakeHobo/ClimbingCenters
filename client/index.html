London Climbing Map

Designed for those visiting London to see & filter centers by 50% student discounts or "super off peak" day availability, especially if their stay is too short to justify a membership. It can also be used to mark and submit new centers or buildering spots by adding them to the CenterDisplay dataset.

Future development ideas include a visual calendar for climbers to mark a set of dates and locations, which alumni may use to plan meet-ups if they wish.
