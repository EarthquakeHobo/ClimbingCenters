Climbing in London is spenny. Indoor boulder gyms cost up to £15 per entry, with monthly memberships costing over £65 per month.

Many gyms however do special "super off peak" discounts for a 50% entry on certain day of the week.

Climbers visiting London for a short stay may benefit from knowing which centers are discounted and when.
