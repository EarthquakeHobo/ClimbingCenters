Climbing in London is spenny. Indoor boulder gyms cost up to £15 per entry, compared to the £8 entry of the fabulous Durham Climbing Center. To climbers visiting London and wanting to visit some of the gyms, it may be best to plan their travels based on the "super off peak" days that many gyms have, giving the option of a 50% day pass.

This website compiles and maps these gyms based on their super off peak days. The dataset is small because I made it myself by visiting their websites/in person.

1. Add and Delete to edit the database (methods Post and Delete). Refresh after Deleting to update Pins.

2. Pin on Map to create new pin (method Patch to edit center.Pin).

3. Find Centers based on discounts (using fetch query)
