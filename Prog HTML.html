Expectations for your first visit

Though the Font grading system (f6, f7A+ etc.) used by many indoor bouldering gyms was indeed named after the Forest, a majority of the problems you'll encounter will feel very difficult for their grade. A strong emphasis on the right beta will be important for sending climbs, and it's generally recommended to give most problems no more than 3 tries before moving on.

Accomodation

3 main options exist, depending on the type of traveller

Cottages
  • Best value accomodation for Families and groups of 4-8 people
  • Private amenities including kitchen, showers, and toilets
  • Most can be booked on Airbnb
  • Almost always a car will be required

Called g√Ætes, these are comfortable cabins of varying sizes that are privately rented out for days to weeks.

Locations can however be relatively remote, and the nearest supermarket may be several miles away.

Hostels
  • Best for solo travellers looking to meet new climber friends
  • Shared amenities including kitchen, showers, and toilets
  • Most within walking distance to at least 2 crags, as well as supermarkets
  • Accessible using public transport/by foot

Font attracts climbers from all around the world, and many stay at hostels for weeks at a time.

Though slightly more expensive than Gites (roughly 25 EUR/night), staying at one is a great way to make new friends.

Camping
  • Best price experienced for travellers
  • Communal amenities including showers and toilets, but no kitchens
  • La Musadiere, one of the better known campsites, is accessible on foot and within walking distance to Trois Pignons

London Climbing Map

Designed for those visiting London to see & filter centers by 50% student discounts or "super off peak" day availability, especially if their stay is too short to justify a membership. It can also be used to mark and submit new centers or buildering spots by adding them to the CenterDisplay dataset.

Future development ideas include a visual calendar for climbers to mark a set of dates and locations, which alumni may use to plan meet-ups if they wish.
